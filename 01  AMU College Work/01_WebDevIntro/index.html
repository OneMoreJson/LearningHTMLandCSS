The Shop

Welcome to C&G!

Mouse Over Me

A place for all the Coffee and Game Gear you need for your most epic D&D quests. We have fun with our game adventures and are always serious about our brews. If you are looking for a black magic elixer to get you through a fifteen hour game session or just wanting some of our Middle Earth famous D20-elicious roasts, you will find it all here at C&G Cafe. Just follow your nose, or the map provided here. We’ll leave the lamp on for you.

The Coffee

We Roast our Own Beans!

Our coffee comes from all over the world. Depending on the season, we might get beans from Colombia, Kona, Mexico, Guatemala or any other wonderful fair-trade plantation. This month, we have an Organic Honduran Marcala that is incredible when lightly roasted. The subtle chocolate flavor pairs wonderfully with our pumkin spice cheesecake. If you are wanting something to put a bit more hair on your paladin’s chest, we recommend the Jamaican Blue Mountain. Roasted dark and served with rich cream, this earthy brew is seriously strong and bold.

The Game

We Love Games!

Come by and feel free to play a game of cards, chess or any number of our stocked board games. Just a warning though, we really love D&D. You could say things get a little rowdy past 6 P.M. We have a come-as-you-are nights every Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday. However Thursday through Friday are D&D adventure leagues with dedicated Dungeon Masters (DMs). You’ll want to sign up as the tables fill up quickly.

The Experience

Letter from Michelle...

Hi, I use to be a closet D&D gamer. My character is a level 14 half elf mage named Rocky. I also really love coffee. One day I was looking for a place to buy some freshly roasted coffee when I came across C&G online. When I visited, the people here were supper kind. The barista gave me a free drink when I bought a bag of their coffee of the month, Dark Rouges Caffeine. I also found a new figurine for my character. I didn’t even know they sell all sorts of D&D gaming stuff. This place is amazing! I feel like I finally found a place I belong!
